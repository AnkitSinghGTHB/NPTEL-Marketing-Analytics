# NPTEL Marketing Analytics - Exam Preparation Guide

Welcome to the ultimate crash course for the **NPTEL Marketing Analytics** exam! 

This guide has been specifically designed to be absorbed in about **10 hours**. It assumes zero prior knowledge, uses simple relatable examples, and highlights the specific "tricks" NPTEL uses in their 100-mark MCQ exams.

## How to use this guide:
1. **Read Modules 1 to 4 sequentially.** They are broken down logically and strip away all the dense mathematical jargon in favor of conceptual understanding.
2. **Review the MEGA CHEATSHEET.** Do this 1 hour before you walk into the exam hall. It condenses everything into rapid-fire formulas and tips.
